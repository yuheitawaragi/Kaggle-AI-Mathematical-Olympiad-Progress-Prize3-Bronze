This repository contains my solution for the Kaggle competition "AI Mathematical Olympiad - Progress Prize 3 (AIMO3)".
The goal of the competition is to develop open-source AI models capable of solving Olympiad-level mathematics problems written in LaTeX format. Participants are required to predict the correct integer answer for challenging problems spanning algebra, geometry, combinatorics, and number theory. The competition aims to advance mathematical reasoning capabilities in large language models (LLMs) and promote open research in AI for mathematics.
